Welcome to Retro Diner: A Timeless Icon

Established in 1952 by Douglas "Duke" Rayson, Retro Diner has been a beloved destination for generations, blending classic American charm with larger-than-life stories. From its beginnings as a car enthusiasts haven to its cosmic pancakes and time-traveling visitors, this family-run diner has become legendary for more than just its food. Now under the care of Lily Rayson, Retro Diner continues to serve comfort food with a side of nostalgia and mystery. [Read More]
