Welcome to Retro Diner: A Timeless Icon

❮
❯

Established in 1952 by Douglas "Duke" Rayson, Retro Diner has been a beloved destination for generations, blending classic American charm with larger-than-life stories. From its vintage chrome exterior to the neon-lit booths inside, Retro Diner has always been more than just a place to eat. It's where car lovers, road-trippers, and curious locals gather to share a meal and swap stories. Each generation of the Rayson family has brought its own unique touch, from Duke's car memorabilia to his son Jack’s “Time Traveler Tuesdays” and cosmic pancakes. Today, Duke's granddaughter Lily keeps the legend alive, adding a modern twist while staying true to the diner’s rich history and mysterious charm.
